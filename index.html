Read Clues Carefully

📋 Task Requirements

  • ENTIRE TEAM required — all appear in photo
  • MINIMUM MEMBERS stated — comply exactly
  • 1 REP — just one person needed
  • If NOT stated — divide & conquer freely

🧠 Be Creative With Proof

  • Use video call screenshots
  • Use reflections (glass/mirror)
  • Creative positioning works
  • Team props or identifiers
  • Plan ahead and regroup

All members must be represented — how you achieve it is your strategy.
